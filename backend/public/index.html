HOW IT WORKS
Three steps. One leash.
01
Register
Give your AI agent an on-chain identity with a spending cap. Only you, as the owner, can set or change the rules. The contract stores them permanently.
02
Enforce
The smart contract checks every transfer request against the cap. If the agent tries to exceed it, the blockchain rejects the transaction. No exceptions.
03
Verify
Every allowed or blocked action produces a deploy hash. Anyone can look it up on the Casper explorer to confirm what happened — and when.
WHY IT MATTERS
The rules live on the blockchain,
not in your code.
🔒
Tamper-proof enforcement
Traditional spending limits live in app code — they can be patched, bypassed, or misconfigured. On Casper, the contract is the enforcer. No code change can override it.
🧾
Immutable audit trail
Every action — allowed or blocked — leaves a permanent record on the public blockchain. You don't need to trust the operator's logs. The chain doesn't lie.
⚡
Real AI integration
Gemini AI reasons in natural language and calls the contract directly. The enforcement still applies. The AI can ask nicely — the blockchain still says no if the cap is exceeded.
🔑
Owner-controlled lifecycle
Register, fund, and revoke agent access at any time. Only the owner's private key can make these changes. Revoked agents are blocked immediately — on-chain, with no grace period.
